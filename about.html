Welcome To Christa Toygers

About Our Cattery

My name is Christina Hershberger "CHRISTA TOYGERS" is a small in home breeder, for 14 years we have had the pleasure of being Toyger Cat owners and breeder. We are TICA registered cattery and proudly follow the TICA Code of Ethics. I have been breeding and raising several types of animals for decades and an animal lover since I was a child. I immediately fell in love with the Toyger cats, and after learning more about the breed I decided to raise these beautiful intelligent animals.
We raise our Toyger kittens for sale in our home with our Four children and our Two friendly dogs. We are dedicated to the breed and are dedicated to producing only the most beautiful, healthiest and highest quality Toyger kittens possible. We work hard to maintain the Toyger Breed Standards, keep track of our bloodlines, treat our kitties with love and respect, and stand behind our health guarantees.

All our kittens and cats are loved and cared for in our home. All our cats are indoor cats, we do not expose them to wild animals. Their health and welfare are our top priority. We believe in a clean and healthy environment for our cats by keeping a very clean and tidy household. Our goal is to breed beautiful, loving, and well socialized cats. We spend quality time with all our cats and kittens to ensure our kittens are well socialized. They are well taught and handled daily starting at a young age, they are well behaved and not aggressive.

Our Cattery Goals

Our cattery goal is to produce healthy & eye catching Toyger kittens for sale with incomparable stripes and well rounded out going personalities. Our focus is improving the overall quality, better type and nicer stripes on our Toyger cats; as we actively participate in TICA cat shows and breed to TICA breed standards. Our cattery proudly follows the TICA Code of Ethics. We work hard to maintain the Toyger Breed Standards, keep track of our bloodlines, treat our kitties with love and respect, and stand behind our health guarantees and all of our adult cats used in our Breeding Program have been genetically tested for PRA and PK Deficiency and received Negative/Negative as a result. We take great pride in the care and health.

I will NEVER breed Toyger kittens for quantity, but instead for quality, health and personality in mind. I follow the TICA Breeders Code of Ethics. All my breeding cats are DNA tested and scanned for Hypertrophic Cardiomyopathy by a cardiologist who comes to my home with the necessary portable equipment. All my Toygers have passed the tests with flying colors. I have more and more kittens with permanent white belly, which is my goal for future litters. Every Toyger kitten has white belly to a certain age, but only the ones born to “whited parents” stay permanent.
