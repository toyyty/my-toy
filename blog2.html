Preparing for your Toyger Kitten

A designated small and comfortable room or space should be selected where your Toyger kitten can spend at least the first one to two weeks, until they are comfortable to explore additional parts of your home. Basic necessities such as food, water, and litter box are also needed. We always send your kitten home with a Quart-size bag of Royal Canin Kitten dry food, the same dry food that is fed at our cattery.
